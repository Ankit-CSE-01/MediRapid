Emergency Basics

Call for Help

Dial emergency services immediately for serious situations:

  • India: 112 or 102 (ambulance)
  • US: 911
  • EU: 112

Assess Safety

Check for dangers before approaching:

  • Traffic or fire hazards
  • Electrical dangers
  • Violent situations

Check Responsiveness

Gently shake and shout at the person:

  • Look for breathing
  • Check for severe bleeding
  • Look for medical alert jewelry
